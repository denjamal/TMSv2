Our Passion

The Meatball Stoppe is the product of over 30+ years in the food industry. Isabella’s Bella Cucina has been in existence since 1992 here in Central Florida, by Isabella Morgia di Vicari. Jeff and Isabella Morgia, co-owners of Isabella’s Bella Cucina Catering, have served people from all walks of life throughout the years, and now this authentic cuisine is available to you!
