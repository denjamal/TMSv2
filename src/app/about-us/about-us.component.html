About Isabella

Isabella Morgia di Vicari is a first-generation American, born of Italian heritage in Brooklyn, NY, with a very large “famiglia” from Calabria and Trieste, Italia. Growing up in Orlando since 1970, raised in her family’s six restaurant businesses which they owned and operated, has taught Isabella many life lessons, including the wonderful culture of food.

About Jeff

Jeff Morgia is CEO and Co-Owner of Isabella’s Bella Cucina and The Meatball Stoppe. Jeff has been in the food industry 30+ years. He spent 12 years with the Sheraton Corporation in several positions within the operations of many hotel locations after graduating from the University of Nevada at Las Vegas.
